Destination of Green Tourism Circuit, Kottayam

Located strategically within the district of Kottayam which always figures in the list of must-see districts of the state, Elaveezha Poonchira and the other thirteen most beautiful destinations in the district together can easily be transformed in to a Tourism Circuit with all the desirable ingredients and qualities, with negligible disturbance to the natural terrain and the original landscape.

Kottayam is the ideal take off point for visits to Peerumed, Munnar, Thekkady, Ernakulam and the temple city of Madurai. It is also a gateway to the pilgrim centers of Sabarimala, Mannanam, Vaikom, Ettumanoor, Bharananganam, Erumeli, Manarcaud, and so on.
