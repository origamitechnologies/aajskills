Tips

Prepare for your trip to Green Kerala with our practical tips. Learn about our geography, currency and how to keep safe.

Emergency Numbers

Police control room: 100
Fire station: 101
Ambulance: 102, 108

Police Helpline

While traveling on Highways (Highway Alert Number): 9846 100 100
While traveling in Trains (Railway Alert Number): 9846 200 100
Website: www.keralapolice.org

Language

Kerala’s official language is Malayalam. However, being a multicultural nation, we also enjoy a tremendous diversity of languages and cultures.

Electrical power points

Our electrical current is 220 – 240 volts, AC 50Hz. The tourists from other countries using different power outlets may need an adaptor.

Banks

Banks are open for transaction from 10:00 - 15:30 hrs on weekdays.

Smoking

Smoking is banned in public places.

Official website

To know more about Kerala, visit the website of the Government of Kerala www.kerala.gov.in
