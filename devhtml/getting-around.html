Getting Around

Green pilgrimage tourist circuit Kottayam, A Nodal agency designated by Government of Kerala for the development of 20 Destinations as tourist attractions in and across Kottayam District. The major activities of the organisation includes

  1. Planning and budgeting of new projects for destination development 
  2. Development of infrastructure for improving accessibility
  3. Advertise and popularise the destinations for increasing social appeal of these destinations
  4. Development of basic amenities for tourists in proposed destinations
  5. Induce investors for investments in development of public infrastructure in accommodation 
  6. Co-ordinate with investors and collecting feedback
  7. Control, Co-ordinate and monitor development of projects in the destinations

GTCK has designed tailor made attractive projects and packages for the development of the proposed tourist destinations and some of these legacy projects is already under development for strengthening the existing infrastructure at some of the destinations.
